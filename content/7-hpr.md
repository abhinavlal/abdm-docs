# Healthcare Professional Registry

Healthcare Professionals Registry is a comprehensive repository of all verified healthcare professionals involved in delivery of healthcare services across both modern and traditional systems of medicine. Enrolling in the Healthcare Professionals Registry will enable them to get connected to Indiaâ€™s digital health ecosystem. 

Currently Doctors and Nurses can enroll in HPR and going forward other categories of professionals will be added.
